Phonetic Shield is KeyForge's unique generator mode. It builds passwords from pronounceable syllable groups — onset consonant + vowel + optional coda — then weaves in high-entropy characters between each syllable. You get a password you can say aloud and commit to memory, yet one that resists dictionary, brute-force, and pattern-based attacks.

Every character is still chosen by the browser's `crypto.getRandomValues()` CSPRNG. Pronounceability comes from the syllable structure, not from any reduction in randomness.

## Syllable structure

Each syllable follows the pattern: **onset + vowel + coda (67% chance)**

```text
[onset] + [vowel] + [coda?]

e.g.  bl  +  ay  +  nd  =  blaYND
      tr  +  oo  +  (none) =  trOO
```

### Onset consonants and blends (37 total)

| Single consonants (17) | Blends (20) |
|---|---|
| b d f g h j k l m n p r s t v w z | bl br cl cr dr fl fr gl gr pl pr sl sm sn sp st sw tr wr sk sc |

### Vowel options (10 total)

| Vowel |
|---|
| a e i o u ay ee oo ai au |

### Coda consonants and blends (22 total)

| Single consonants (14) | Blends (8) |
|---|---|
| b d f g k l m n p r s t x z | nd nt st ck mp nk sk sp |

The coda is added with a **67% probability** per syllable (`cryptoRand(3) > 0`), keeping passwords varied in length even at the same syllable count.

## Settings reference

<ParamField path="options.syllableCount" type="number" default="3">
  Number of syllable groups. Range: 2–6. More syllables mean a longer, higher-entropy password.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="options.entropyLevel" type="string" default="medium">
  Controls what is injected between syllables and at the end. Options: `light`, `medium`, `heavy`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="options.capitalize" type="boolean" default="true">
  Capitalise the first letter of each syllable for visual separation.
</ParamField>

## Entropy injection levels

Entropy characters are inserted **between every pair of syllables** and **once at the very end** (for policy compliance). The total number of injection points is always `syllableCount` — one between each adjacent pair plus one at the end.

| Level | Characters injected | Source pools |
|---|---|---|
| Light | 1 symbol | `!@#$%^&*_+-` (11 symbols) |
| Medium | 1 symbol + 1 digit (order randomised) | 11 symbols + `23456789` (8 digits) |
| Heavy | symbol + digit + symbol | 11 symbols + 8 digits |

<Info>
The digits `0` and `1` are excluded from injection to avoid confusion with the letters `O` and `l`. The injection digit pool is `23456789` (8 options).
</Info>

For medium level, the symbol and digit order is itself randomised per injection point — `symbol+digit` or `digit+symbol` with equal probability.

## Pronunciation guide

Every generated result includes a `guide` string alongside the `password`:

```text
password:  "Blaind#7Groo+Smek!"
guide:     "BLAIND · GROO · SMEK"
```

The guide shows the raw syllables in uppercase, separated by ` · `, with all injected entropy characters stripped out. Enable **Show pronunciation guide** in the UI to see it displayed below the output field.

This lets you read a complex password aloud without having to spell each character: say the syllable sounds, then enter the injected characters when needed.

## Entropy calculation

<Info>
Phonetic entropy is estimated as: `syllableCount × log₂(sylSpace) + injections × injectedChars × log₂(symPool + digPool)`

where `sylSpace = 39 × 10 × 17 + 39 × 10 ≈ 7020` (onset × vowel × coda variants including no-coda cases), `injections = syllableCount`, `symPool = 11`, `digPool = 8`.
</Info>

Approximate entropy by syllable count and injection level:

| Syllables | Light (bits) | Medium (bits) | Heavy (bits) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | ~43 | ~56 | ~69 |
| 3 | ~65 | ~84 | ~104 |
| 4 | ~87 | ~112 | ~138 |
| 5 | ~108 | ~140 | ~173 |
| 6 | ~130 | ~168 | ~207 |

At 3 syllables with medium injection you reach ≈ 84 bits — **Very Strong** territory.

## Example output

```text
# 3 syllables, medium, capitalize on
password:  Blaind*4Greem+7Smekt!
guide:     BLAIND · GREEM · SMEKT

# 2 syllables, light, capitalize off
password:  troot!drask!
guide:     TROOT · DRASK

# 4 syllables, heavy, capitalize on
password:  Floot#3@Bren!7*Spail$6!Kroot@8#
guide:     FLOOT · BREN · SPAIL · KROOT
```
